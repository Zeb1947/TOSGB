# The Old School Guidebook™ (TOSGB)

**Retro tech guides & nostalgic tips**

---

## About

The Old School Guidebook (TOSGB) is a website dedicated to preserving and sharing the knowledge of retro technology, old-school software, and nostalgic computing experiences.  

The goal is to provide clear, step-by-step guides presented in a classic, retro style — simple layouts, screenshots, and instructions that anyone can follow.  

TOSGB emphasizes accessibility and clarity while maintaining a nostalgic aesthetic reminiscent of older computing guides and manuals.

---

## Purpose

- **Preserve retro knowledge:** Document processes, software, and hardware that might otherwise be forgotten.  
- **Educate and guide:** Make it easy for beginners and enthusiasts to follow along.  
- **Maintain a retro feel:** Design and style reflect the era of old-school computing.  
- **Accessible and expandable:** Easy to add new guides and resources over time without losing the classic style.
